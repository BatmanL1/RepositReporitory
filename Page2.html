How gaming was in my interest

My father loved to play games as much as I do now. Some of his favorite games were street fighter, afro samurai and call of duty. I woke up one day and saw him playing one of the games and I was so immersed. Iwanted to play too but I knew i was too young. I remember one time I thought my dad was letting me play when really he gave me a controller that had no batteries in it. I wanted to play fighting games so I asked my mom if I could get some on my phone she let me get some and this one game had me hooked. It was Call of Mini Infinity . I was like Halo and Call of Duty combined into one multiplayer game. Another thing is that there were tons of modders and hackers in the game. But that didnt stop me because there were some pretty well known glitches that would give you tons of things or limited time items. I played that game for so long it was like second nature hitting headshots all the time. This is what started getting me into the genre of shooting games.
